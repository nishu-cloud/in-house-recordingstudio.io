About the Studio

Born from
the Underground

In-House Recording Studio is Pune's most raw and authentic independent recording space — built by artists, for artists. We don't just record music; we capture energy.

From hip-hop to punk, emo rap to indie, our walls are soaked in culture. The iconic cigarette-box heart installation and artist posters covering every corner aren't just decor — they're a statement.

Located in Laxmi Chowk, Hinjawadi Phase 1 Pune, we bring professional-grade recording accessible to every local artist ready to make noise.
